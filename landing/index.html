CSR Generator

Generate RFC-compliant Certificate Signing Requests with SANs, key usage fields, and your choice of RSA or ECC keys — ready to submit to any CA.

Chain Builder

Drop in a server cert and get a complete, ordered chain back — root, intermediates, and leaf — automatically assembled from your system's trust store.

Custom Chain Builder

Manually assemble a chain from your own files. Supply certs in any order — PRISM validates and outputs a clean, correctly ordered PEM bundle.

PFX / P12 Generator

Bundle your certificate chain and private key into a password-protected PFX archive ready for IIS, F5, Palo Alto, and more.

Key Extractor

Pull private keys and certificates out of any PFX/P12 file in one step. No OpenSSL commands to remember.

Crypt Walk

Inspect any cert file at a glance — subject, issuer, SANs, validity dates, key algorithm, and more. Instantly know what you're working with.

ClearPass Deploy

Push certificates directly to Aruba ClearPass over the REST API — discover servers, select a service, and upload without leaving the app.
